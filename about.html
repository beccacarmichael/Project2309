Meet The Team

The LYB Consulting Group consists of three pivotal members who are dedicated to providing high-quality web-based GIS solutions for the client. Each team member of LYB Consulting brings their unique experience to the table in order to deliver the best product as outlined below

Lucija Bralic

Lucija’s background in coding makes her the perfect fit for the role of Lead Programmer. She has always been interested in playing with code and finding solutions to complex problems. During her time spent at Fleming College, she has gained valuable skills in automation using Python, and has applied these skills in several major projects. She also has experience using Arcade to customize Dashboards in ArcGIS Online, in addition to using JavaScript libraries such as Leaflet to create open-source web solutions. In addition, with a BSc in Biology and Environmental Sciences, she has a very good understanding of the ecological applications for this project. She is confident that she can use these skills and experiences to support the team in creating a successful product for the client.
Yingjia Ye

Yingjia obtained decent grades in Database courses during her time spent in the GIS program at Fleming College. She has gained experience in using both Microsoft Access and PostgreSQL in various class projects. Additionally, she is familiar with extracting, transforming, and loading data using FME. She has also gained experience in Python scripting and data visualization in other classes. She is confident in her ability to apply the skills she learned from class and make valuable contributions to support the team.
Becca Carmichael

Becca’s background in Environmental Geography and Ecosystem Management gives her an understanding of the importance of wetland conservation and communicating it with the public. Her professional experience also gives her the communication skills to help organize the team and liaise with external members of the project in order to ensure the project is on track and the deliverables align with the client's vision. Throughout the GIS Application Specialist Program Becca has developed an understanding in data management, database creation and scripting which she will be able to apply toward the completion of this project. She has also further developed her skills in design and visual communication which she will use to ensure the final deliverables feature aesthetic and accessible designs and cartographic outputs.
