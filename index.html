Immersive Insights Into Nature: Environmental Monitoring Dashboards for Kawartha Conservation

About The Project

As a part of their duty to ensure the ongoing health of the watershed, the Integrated Watershed Management department conducted a review of the Environmental Programs currently in operation which lead to the development of a 10-year Environmental Monitoring Strategy beginning in 2022. This Strategy outlined many recommendations including; to report data regularly, to track land use changes and to establish a data sharing platform which is the stepping-stone for this project.

The goal of the Immersive Insights Into Nature project is to create interactive Dashboards for select Environmental Programs aimed at achieving those recommendations.

The project goal was achieved by creating several public-facing web solutions that tell a story about the 3 selected Environmental Monitoring Programs  Biomonitoring  Temperature monitoring  And water quality monitoring

Upon project completion, these solutions will be utilized on Kawartha Conservation’s corporate website to display UpToDate data and findings about the watershed. Allowing public interaction and utilization and the ongoing success of the Environmental Monitoring Strategy.

The Client

The Client for this project is the Integrated Watershed Management Department at Kawartha Conservation which is located in Ontario Canada overlapping the following municipalities;  the City of Kawartha Lakes  the Township of Scugog  the Township of Brock  the Municipality of Clarington  the Municipality of Trent Lakes  the Township of Cavan Monaghan  the Mississaugas of Scugog Island First Nation

Kawartha Conservation is one of 36 conservation authorities operating in Ontario and a member of Conservation Ontario. They maintain a base level of environmental monitoring in order to track and monitor changes to watershed health overtime
