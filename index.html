Immersive Insights Into Nature: Environmental Monitoring Dashboards for Kawartha Conservation

Project 2309 Created by LYB Consulting for Kawartha Conservation

Overview

Kawartha Conservation, located in Ontario Canada, is one of 36 conservation authorities operating in Ontario and a member of Conservation Ontario. As a part of doing their duty to ensure the ongoing health of the watershed they developed a 10-year Environmental Monitoring Strategy which included many recommendations some of which are centered around data reporting and sharing to encourage public interaction and understanding of the vital information Kawartha Conservation collects throughout the watershed. To achieve these recommendations Kawartha Conservation partnered with LYB Consulting and the Immersive Insights Into Nature: Environmental Monitoring Dashboards project was developed.

Our team has worked to develop several resources Kawartha Conservation can utilize to achieve these data sharing recommendations for three of their Environmental Programs: Biomonitoring, Water Temperature Monitoring, and Water Quality Monitoring. We have created individual Dashboards for each Environmental Program as well as a StoryMap housing those Dashboards. Kawartha Conservation will add these to their public facing website, allowing the public to interact with the data and observe the findings of each Environmental Program in an immersive way. During the creation of this project the team also insured the data could be updated frequently by the client through an automated process in order to efficiently keep the data presented up to date.
